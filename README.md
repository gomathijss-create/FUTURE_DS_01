# **Business Sales Performance Analytics**

## 📊 Project objective

To analyse business sales data and identify sales trends, profitable categories, 
regional performance, and loss-making products in order to support data-driven 
business decisions and recommend growth strategies.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

The following tools and libraries are required to run this project:

* Python 3.x
* colab notebook
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel / CSV

Install required libraries using:

pip install pandas matplotlib seaborn

### Project Insights

The analysis provides insights such as:

* Technology category generated the highest sales (USD 836K) and profit 
(USD 145K), making it the strongest performing business segment. 

* Sales showed a consistent upward trend from 2015 to 2017, with the 
highest sales recorded in 2017 (USD 733K), indicating steady business 
growth. 

* The West region contributed the highest sales (USD 725K) and profit 
(USD 108K), making it the most profitable geographical market.

* Phones, Chairs, and Storage emerged as the top revenue-generating sub
categories, significantly contributing to overall business performance. 

* Tables, Bookcases, and Supplies were identified as loss-making sub
categories, with Tables generating the highest loss (USD -17.7K). 

## 💡 Recommendations

*  Increase investment and marketing efforts in Technology products to maximize 
revenue and profitability.

*  Focus on expanding operations and customer acquisition strategies in the West 
region while replicating its successful practices in other regions.

* Review pricing strategies, discount policies, and supply chain costs for Tables 
and Bookcases to reduce losses.

* Optimize Furniture category profitability through better cost management and 
discount control, as it generates high sales but relatively low profit.

*  Maintain sufficient inventory and promotional campaigns for high-performing 
products such as Phones, Chairs, and Storage to sustain business growth. 

## Conclusion

The business demonstrates strong growth potential with increasing sales trends 
and high-performing Technology products. However, loss-making sub-categories 
such as Tables and Bookcases require strategic attention. Implementing data
driven decisions can help improve profitability and support sustainable business 
growth.
